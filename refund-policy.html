Refund & Cancellation Policy

      Rules regarding refund requests, subscriptions, and tournament cancellations

      Bidar Chess Association Refund Policy

      Effective Date: 25/10/2024

      At Bidar Chess Association, we strive to provide quality chess workshops, tournaments, events, and seminars. We understand that plans may change, and we have outlined our Refund and Cancellation Policy to ensure transparency and fairness for all participants.

      1. Workshop, Seminar, and Event Fees

      Cancellation by Participant:

      • Cancellations made 7 days or more before the event/workshop date will be eligible for a full refund, minus any applicable processing fees.
      • Cancellations made within 3-6 days before the event/workshop date will be eligible for a 50% refund.
      • Cancellations made within 48 hours of the event/workshop will not be eligible for a refund.

      Cancellation by Bidar Chess Association:

      • If an event, workshop, or seminar is canceled by Bidar Chess Association, participants will receive a full refund or the option to transfer their registration to a future event.

      2. Chess Tournaments

      • Entry Fees are Non-Refundable unless the tournament is canceled by the Bidar Chess Association.
      • In case of a rescheduled tournament, participants can either request a full refund or transfer their entry to the new date.

      3. Membership and Subscription Fees

      • All membership and subscription fees are non-refundable once paid.
      • Memberships are valid for the designated period and cannot be transferred or prorated.

      4. Refund Processing

      • Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days via the original mode of payment.
      • Any applicable bank or transaction charges will be deducted from the refund amount.

      Bidar Chess Association reserves the right to update or modify this policy as necessary. Any changes will be communicated through our official channels. Thank you for your support and understanding. We appreciate your participation in our chess initiatives!
